# Make finding and adopting a rescue dog in the UK effortless

**Scope:** Personal
**Timeframe:** Q3 2026 (Jul–Sep)
**Confidence at draft:** 7/10

## Objective

Make finding and adopting a rescue dog in the UK effortless by bringing every rescue's dogs into one place. Q3 establishes the foundation by achieving complete coverage of ADCH-registered rescues in Wales.

## Notes

- **"Aggregated" definition:** a rescue counts toward KR1 only when consent is obtained AND its available dogs are live, customer-facing, and refreshed on a regular cadence (stale listings don't count). Set the exact refresh bar (e.g. ≥ weekly) when reporting is built.
- **KR1 dependencies:** (1) obtain the definitive list of ADCH-registered Welsh rescues to establish the denominator; (2) build coverage reporting (X of N rescues live, with last-refresh timestamps); (3) build a consent/onboarding process for rescues (how you ask, what they agree to, data-sharing terms). Scraping without consent is a relationship and legal/terms risk — consent is part of the bar, not optional.
- **KR2 dependency:** web analytics (e.g. Plausible) needs setting up. Target of 100/month is provisional — refine once a launch traffic baseline exists.
- **KR3 dependency:** outbound click tracking needs building. Target of 1/month is a deliberate "first sign of life" validation bar — raise it once there is traffic.
- True completed adoptions are intentionally not measured directly (they complete on the rescue's side and are hard to attribute); KR3 measures the qualified hand-off instead.
