# Intent inbox

The upstream of my OKR suite — raw intent before it's a committed objective. This is the
**INTENT** stage of [the Method Loop](../docs/method.md): what I want to be true and why,
captured before it's shaped into measurable goals.

The `/intent` skill owns this file. `discover` surfaces and appends intents here;
`prioritise` ranks them, promotes the top ones into OKRs under `okrs/active/` (via `/okr`),
and flips their status to `promoted`.

**Status:** `raw` (just captured) → `prioritised` (selected for the current suite) →
`promoted` (now a live OKR; linked).
